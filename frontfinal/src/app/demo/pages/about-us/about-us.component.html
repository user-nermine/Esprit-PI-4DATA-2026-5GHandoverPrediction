Alert priority rulesTS 38.331 · TS 38.133 · TS 38.214
Final alert priority is determined by combining radio thresholds with model predictions in real time.
P1 Critical
RSRP ≤ −110 dBm OR SINR ≤ 0 dB OR HO probability ≥ 0.70
Immediate action
P2 High
RSRP ≤ −100 dBm OR HO probability ≥ 0.50 OR signal drop
Active monitoring
P3 Medium
SINR ≤ 8 dB OR UE velocity > 100 km/h
Informational
Evaluated by the monitoring service every 5 seconds · Real-time inference via trained models · Simulator pushes data every 3 seconds
